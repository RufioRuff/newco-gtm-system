# Platform Gatekeeper Pitch Template

## Meeting with {{name}} - {{company}}

### Meeting Objective
Position NEWCO as an emerging manager fund-of-funds worth tracking, build relationship for future platform inclusion.

### Key Messages

**1. The Opportunity (2 min)**
- Emerging managers (< $250M AUM) outperform by 300-500 bps
- Access challenge: Institutional LPs struggle with direct emerging manager investing
- Our solution: Professional fund-of-funds with institutional infrastructure

**2. Our Differentiation (3 min)**
- **Fee transparency**: 1.25% management fee (no carry) vs. traditional 2/20
- **Liquidity**: Quarterly redemptions vs. 10-year lockups
- **Focus**: 15-20 high-conviction managers, rigorous selection
- **Platform value-add**: Omniscient AI-powered deal flow and analytics

**3. Why This Matters to {{company}} (5 min)**
- Emerging manager access is a growing client need
- Fee pressure is real - our structure addresses it directly
- Educational opportunity: We're tracking the emerging manager landscape closely
- Potential platform fit: {{specific_platform_angle}}

### Discussion Topics

**Manager Selection:**
- Our due diligence process
- How we identify high-potential emerging managers
- Track record verification and reference checks

**Fee Structure:**
- Why we chose 1.25% (alignment, transparency, competitiveness)
- How it affects manager selection and fund economics
- LP reception so far

**Market Opportunity:**
- Emerging manager landscape in 2026
- Platform trends {{company}} is seeing
- Institutional appetite for this vehicle type

### Questions for Them

1. What's {{company}}'s current view on emerging manager investing?
2. How do clients typically access emerging managers today?
3. What would make a fund-of-funds attractive for platform inclusion?
4. Timeline and process for platform evaluation?
5. Other funds in this space they're tracking?

### Ask

- **Primary:** Stay in touch as we build track record, revisit platform inclusion in 12-18 months
- **Secondary:** Introductions to 2-3 LPs who might be interested in emerging manager exposure
- **Tertiary:** Periodic check-ins to share market insights

### Follow-up Materials
- One-pager on NEWCO
- Manager selection criteria doc
- Emerging manager performance data
