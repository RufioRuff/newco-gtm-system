
**Schema:**
- `company_id` - Unique identifier
- `company_name` - Company name
- `fund_id` - Parent fund ID
- `fund_name` - Parent fund name
- `gp_name` - General partner name
- `sector` - Industry sector
- `stage` - Investment stage
- `status` - Active or Exited
- `investment_date` - When fund invested
- `exit_date` - When company exited (if applicable)
- `exit_type` - IPO or Acquisition
- `valuation_estimate` - Last known valuation
- `description` - What the company does
